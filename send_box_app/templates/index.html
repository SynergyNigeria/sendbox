Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our shipping services. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly.

Delivery times vary depending on the destination and service selected. Local deliveries typically take 1-3 business days, while international shipping can take 3-14 business days. Express services are available for faster delivery.

Our pricing depends on package weight, dimensions, destination, and service level. We offer competitive rates starting from $50 for local deliveries. International shipping starts at $200. Get a free quote by contacting us or using our online calculator.

Once your package is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your package in real-time on our website using the tracking number, or through our mobile app. Updates are provided at every major milestone.

We cannot ship hazardous materials, flammable liquids, illegal substances, perishable items, live animals, or items prohibited by international regulations. For a complete list of restricted items, please review our shipping guidelines or contact our team.

Yes, we offer comprehensive package insurance for added protection. Coverage starts at 1% of the declared value (minimum $10). Additional coverage is available for high-value shipments. Insurance claims are processed within 30 days of filing.

If your package arrives damaged, please take photos of the damage and packaging before signing for delivery. Contact our customer service within 48 hours with your tracking number and photos. We'll investigate and process compensation according to our insurance policy.
